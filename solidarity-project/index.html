Solidarity
16 December 2021

Solidarity project

Thanks to your generosity, we managed to collect 706 kg of rice and many toys. Everything was delivered to the Huai Yai town hall with the help of some of the EFIP students who were able to meet the mayor and his deputies. The distribution has started and has already made many happy, it will continue in the days to come.

Thank you to the students, parents and teachers who participated in this action of solidarity.

EFIP wishes you a very good vacation season and a happy new year!
